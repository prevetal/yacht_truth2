How We Operate

We start with a meeting where we define objectives, constraints and success metrics. We assemble the right team and set a clear work plan. With the brief in hand, we run a full options review and go through a tender process, visiting assets and verifying assumptions. All scenarios are compared on cost, risk and outcome, with the best path is selected for the project.
